Rules-based IT operations cost businesses hundreds of billions of dollars every year. If your company depends on human labor to paper over where partial IT automation falls short, you know how much it costs to keep systems online, access controlled, and users properly provisioned.

Agentic AI offers a better path forward. Instead of relying on brittle, partial automation, modern companies can use AI agents to keep their technology running smoothly with less human input. The result? Less downtime, faster service, and lower costs.

But, as always, there’s a catch. Agentic AI is no panacea on its own. If you want to use agentic AI to automate and accelerate your IT operations, you need to get your data in order. Today, that information may be siloed, trapped in different repositories that your IT team has to correlate by hand to understand. Worse, your team has knowledge that is not well represented by existing databases.

This is the precise challenge [BigPanda](https://www.bigpanda.io/) is attacking with its [IT Knowledge Graph](https://www.bigpanda.io/our-product/it-knowledge-graph/). The tool connects data sources (your CMDB, observability data, etc.) with your team’s knowledge (runbooks, SOPs), with AI-powered sensing atop both to spot issues before they metastasize. By combining traditional ITops data and human knowledge, BigPanda’s IT Knowledge Graph gives AI agents the information they need and the context only people can provide.
